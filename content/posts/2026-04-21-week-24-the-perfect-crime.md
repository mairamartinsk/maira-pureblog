- 🍅 Luna ate ALL the tomatoes I had left on the counter (allegedly!). A friend was here recently claiming how tomatoes are so much more delicious if left outside the fridge and I fell for it. Even though my tomato plants in the garden have literal fences around to protect them from the monster, I still fell for it and left the tomatoes outside, on the counter, right where she could get them. And suddenly they were not there anymore. I don't have any proof it was her, but it has to be. She ate almost 1kg of tomatoes (500g cherry tomatoes and 4 or 5 *tomate pera*). Once again, a perfect crime[^luna] with no evidence, not even a fart afterwards.

[^luna]: Every single tomato I've ever planted before the fences were installed, disappeared before I could harvest them. Same with the strawberries, I spent a year waiting for them, and then there were none. Also an entire box of butter cookies I gave to my MIL on her birthday, POOF! And about half a kilo of **raw** rice, mysteriously gone! I found some rice grains around the living room sofa, but nothing conclusive. The hardened criminal stole and ate 1.5kgs of meat entraña for BBQ right under our noses, and no one saw a thing... My 18h slow rise rye bread was left for a minute on the counter while I turned on the oven, and it magically disappeared. *Coincidentally*, on this very day, the dog showed up with an enormous round belly. After a vet visit we even nicknamed her Balloona. Again, we don't have any proof. The crimes happen really fast and there's never a breadcrumb left, no pawprints, nothing to connect her to the crime scene. It's all circumstantial.
