Discovering Christine Donkin earlier this year was a game changer for me. I love studying classical music and I love playing recognizable pop/rock tunes, but what I didn't know is that I truly LOVE fantasy/adventure/cinematic pieces by unknown (to me) contemporary composers.

After listening to a few of her pieces on youtube, I quickly bought her [Piano Pieces Vol. I: Elementary](https://www.amazon.com/Piano-Pieces-Elementary-Christine-Donkin/dp/B09FCB479Y/), a collection of three books in one volume with levels ranging from prep to grade 3.

The pieces are easy enough, yet quite challenging — I'm done with the first book ([Jumping in the Mud](/jumping-in-the-mud-christine-donkin)) and working through the second one (**Comics & Card Tricks**), and even the most basic preparatory pieces require musicality, artistry and good technique. I find them super motivating!
