Who would have thunk I'd be able to learn this?! 😀 Seven months into my journey, I'm playing Scott Joplin's "The Entertainer". Well, a simplified arrangement by the Fabers, but still.

Yes, I know I play it *extremely slow*. I don't feel confident trying to play anything at a fast pace. My fingers can't keep up, the whole piece collapses. 💩

The intro is not in the arrangement. My husband taught me — he plays the piano really well and can play the original score of this piece (at the right tempo too).
