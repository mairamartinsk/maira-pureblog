- 🤧💉 Did a prick allergy test! Allergic to all kinds of pollen and stratospheric levels of dust mites allergy! Doctor thinks the immunization shots would be nice in my case.
- 🌶️ Bought a [Peugeot Paris pepper mill](https://www.amazon.es/dp/B000RAC7U4) and I'm in love! I didn't know what I was missing. Never again will a grocery store pepper grinder enter my kitchen.
- 🎄 We got an [Escape the Room Advent Calendar book](https://www.amazon.es/dp/B0FNQYDKC8) this year again. Each day after lunch we solve one puzzle over coffee and chocolate :)
- 🐶 Astro was doing so well, he was walking faster, getting up more easily, he was so happy, always bringing his toys and ready for a walk. Then, last week, Luna jumped and landed directly on his back! He was in so much pain, he could barely manage a few steps, he lost his appetite. It really broke my heart. After several days of rest, pain medicine and heat treatment (my electric blanket), he's almost fully recovered.
- 🧦 I got Astro some [anti-slippery socks](https://www.amazon.es/dp/B0FNQY14KK) for the house. Sometimes he struggles so much to get himself up — it's hard enough with just the osteoarthritis, and our cold, slippery ceramic floor is not helping. The socks are wonderful, they work like a charm!
- ☎️ My Xiaomi 15 Ultra arrived and it is **amazing**! HyperOS is really nice, so many cool features and smooth animations. The camera is a beast, the Leica magic is truly there. Looking forward to taking it out on day trips and comparing it to my Fuji!
