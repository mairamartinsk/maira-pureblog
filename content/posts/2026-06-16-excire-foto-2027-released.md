Excire Foto 2027 was just released and it has one feature I really love and miss from my LR days: **Map View**.

My current version (Excire Foto 2025), which I just bought three months ago, does [everything I want and need](/dam-excire-foto-2025), but I'm very tempted to upgrade. Also: their Map View is powered by OpenStreetMaps, which is kind of a big deal for all of us trying to degoogle!
