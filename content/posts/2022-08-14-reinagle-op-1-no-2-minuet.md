I recently bought the Schott edition of Reinagle's Op. 1: "24 Short and Easy Pieces" and have been going through all the charming 18th century miniatures in order. I'd consider them more like short études rather than actual repertoire pieces, progressing from preparatory/début level up to a grade 1.
