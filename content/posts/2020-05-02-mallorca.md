A small bed & breakfast inland and a rented car to explore the island. One full week of Mallorca! 🗺️ This beach is called _Cala Agulla_ and it really was as blue as it looks in the image.

![People relax on a sandy beach under a clear blue sky, with some shade provided by a large tree.](/content/images/2020-05-02/2c1d069ec6.jpg)

The view from the road, arriving in _Sa Calobra_. Breathtaking.

![A stunning coastal view features clear blue waters framed by rugged cliffs and lush greenery.](/content/images/2020-05-02/ac82eb1841.jpg)

Last, but not least: Ryanair cancelled our flight back home, the island was crowded and they couldn't find any cheap accomodation near the airport, so we were _forced_ to overnight at this incredible luxury hotel with a view to the sea, and had and amazing dinner including a bottle of wine, all expenses paid by RyanAir! I’ve never been this lucky, before or after.
