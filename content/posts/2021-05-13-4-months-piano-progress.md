Four months learning piano! My progress has slowed down a little this past month, because of work and family. Still, I continue practicing every single day and I can really feel the benefits of consistent, mindful practice. My fingers are more agile, my tempo is more accurate, I can hear better the notes.

Here's *Un bel dì, vedremo* (One fine day we'll see), an aria from the opera Madama Butterfly, by Giacomo Puccini.

The sheet music is FREE, courtesy of [MakingMusicFun](https://www.makingmusicfun.net/htm/f_printit_free_printable_sheet_music/madame-butterfly-piano).
