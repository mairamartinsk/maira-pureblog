I just started working on Kabalevsky's "24 Pieces for Children", Op. 39. This is the very first piece in the album, "A little melody" or "A little tune".

Dmitry Kabalevsky (1904 — 1987) was a Russian/Soviet pianist and one of the most prolific composers of his time. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dmitry_Kabalevsky) has an extensive biography.

In terms of **sheet music**, there are quite a few editions of Opus 39 in [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=kabalevsky+op+39&crid=FC039WPAN8E1&sprefix=kabalevsky+op+39%2Caps%2C178&ref=nb_sb_noss_1) to choose from.
