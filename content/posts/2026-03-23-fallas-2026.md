We go to Fallas every single year since we moved to Valencia. We always pick one or two days to go and see as many fallas as we can. I love the mascletà, so always try to see at least a couple too. We rarely go during the night, because of our dogs.

My mother-in-law's birthday is right around Fallas, so we turned the festival into part of her birthday celebrations! 🎉
