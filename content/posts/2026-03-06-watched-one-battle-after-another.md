Finally watched [One Battle After Another](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/1054867-one-battle-after-another).

We almost gave up after the first 30 minutes or so, the dynamic between the military guy and the revolutionary girl just wasn't there, the acting was poor, the video editing was off, I don't know. I'm glad we never give up on movies, though, as the remaining 130 minutes were amazing!
