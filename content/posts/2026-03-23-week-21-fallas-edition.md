Better one week later than never. This "week" covers March 9 to 22 and the entire period was basically all one big birthday celebration.

- My mother-in-law turned 74 a week ago and she wasn't super happy about it. So we decided we had to start celebrating a few days prior and continue for a full week afterwards. 🎉 On the day off, we sneaked out of the house super early to buy the most beautiful bouquet of fresh flowers, then went for lunch on a wonderful michelin-recommended [restaurant](https://www.restaurante-apicius.com/). The week was full of music, restaurants, tapas, good wine, smiles and happiness, as it should be! 💐
- **FALLAS!!!** This year we finally saw it all, mascletà, cremà, nit del foc, ofrenda, everything. MIL was absolutely stunned and in love with the festival :) 🎆
- We normally have [almuerzo](https://viajes.nationalgeographic.com.es/gastronomia/l-esmorzaret-religion-brunch-a-valenciana_15294) (Valencian brunch) at a local bar in our pueblo. This weekend, still in celebratory mode, we decided to get fancy and drove to [Villa Indiano](https://villaindiano.com/en/). Super hipster, lol, the food is overrated, but the weather was nice and the garden is wonderful. 🍺
- We took Luna for a walk around Salto de la Novia, a 30m tall waterfall near Valencia. I miss [Astro](/goodbye-astro) a lot, but it is so good to be able to take the car and go for long walks in nature! 🏞️
