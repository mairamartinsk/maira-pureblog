Back to proper weeks that last seven days. This one spans March 2 - 8.

- Bought the Canon Selphy CP1500 printer to print photos of Astro, but I have been printing everything: tiny photos for my illustrated journal, random moments, smartphone shots of my friends. My DxO edits benefitted from ICC profiles, but smartphone photos look pretty awesome. 📷
- Finally edited and printed 100 photos of my wedding, **11 years** after the fact, lol! I think we looked at the images *once* back in 2015. Well, now I have a little wedding album that we can show to others and that we can look at whenever we feel like – even if there's no electricity or internet. The magic of analog! ❤️
- I think an update broke my Stremio + Real Debrid setup completely. We literally watched half a movie on Thursday, took a bathroom break and have been unable to watch anything since then. Spent two nights troubleshooting, frustrated. Now I'm planning to roll back the Stremio version. 🏴‍☠️
- Two dear [friends who live full-time in a camper van](https://www.instagram.com/dependedojeitoquevoceve/) stopped by this week. We hadn't seen them in almost a year! We had pasta and too much wine, heard crazy stories, and laughed so hard until two in the morning. I love when they stop for a visit! 🎉
- After a couple weeks of rain and grey weather, we finally got our Valencia sunshine back. Yesterday we went with Luna and my mother-in-law for a walk around Ruta del Agua, in Chelva. Everything was green, the air smelled fresh, the river was crystal clear.🌳
