I'm still committed to Piano Career Academy ([PCA](https://www.pianocareeracademy.com/)) and the Russian School of Piano Playing (RSPP) method book, but I have started using Faber Piano Adventures again. The Russian book is amazing, but I really missed playing those corny Faber arrangements of famous pieces :)
