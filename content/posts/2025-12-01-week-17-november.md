- 🚗 I started my driving lessons! AAAAAAAAAH!
- 🎨 Gifted myself a [Disney coloring book](https://www.hachetteheroes.com/produit/85487/9782017285649/coloriages-mysteres-disney-grands-classiques-special-debutants), a set of [120 Castle Arts colored pencils](https://es.castlearts.com/en/collections/coloured-pencil-sets/products/120-piece-coloured-pencil-set-in-zip-up-case) (wonderful), a box of [150 Crayola Super Tips](https://www.crayola.com/products/markers/super-tips-washable-markers-150-count-585151) water-based markers and [24 Crayola Colors of the World markers](https://www.crayola.com/products/markers/colors-of-the-world-washable-skin-tone-markers-24-count-587802).
- 🫟 I debated long about buying the more expensive alcohol markers, but I just don't know if I'll stick to coloring books for more than 2 months… So I bought the cheap crayolas and that will scratch the itch! Future purchase plans include acrylic markers and tempera sticks for my art journal!
- 🖼️ Speaking on it, I'm a bit intimidated by my illustrated journal / art journal / dear diary / sketchbook. As ridiculous as it sounds, I'm afraid of messing up my own journal that no one will ever see. I overthink it, I don't know if I should just doodle my day, of if I should use watercolors, if it's ok to use it for random art experiments, if it's a waste of good paper to simply write some days… Getting myself anxious over such a dumb thing. I just realized, this very second, that this is just me avoiding the anxiety of other things (trading, learning to drive, insomnia, dog getting old etc). I'll try to be more open-minded and playful with it.

Co-engineered with Leica, the biggest sensor of all smartphones out there… It is a big deal and a photographer's dream. And it will be mine in a few days!
