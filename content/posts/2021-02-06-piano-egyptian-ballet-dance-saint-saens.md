This is the first piece I worked on that is not from my Adult Piano Adventures method book! *Egyptian Ballet Dance* is an easy arrangement of Danse Bacchanale from the opera "Samson & Delilah", by Camille Saint-Saëns.

The sheet music is in Faber Studio Collection - Selections from Showtime Piano (Level 2A).
