## Drawing {#drawing}

If you are in Europe, head to a local or online art supply store, instead of Amazon. Everything is cheaper on the real shops than *chez* Bezos!

- [Royal Talens Art Creation Sketchbook](https://www.royaltalens.com/collections/talens-art-creation-sketchbooks-paper) - Love the A5 size, love the 140/gsm paper, love the price, love that they work for mixed media and even light watercolors. 
- **Waterproof ink pens**: Uniball eye/vision micro, Uniball uni pin fineliners, and Pigma microns. I have several in different sizes, for sketching.
- **Fountain pens**: Lamy Safari, Pilot Kaküno and Platinum Preppy, all with F nibs. I use them for daily journaling.
- [Winsor & Newton Cotman Sketchers' Pocket Set](https://uk.winsornewton.com/collections/watercolour-sets/products/cotman-watercolours-sketchers-pocket-box) with 12 half pans - I like this a lot. The little brush is useless.
- [Escoda Versàtil](https://escoda.com/en/product-category/watercolour/versatil-watercolour/) watercolor brushes in sizes 2, 6 and 10. Absolutely amazing, worth every cent.

## Fitness {#fitness}

Once our local gym closed because of the covid pandemic, we invested in home-gym equipment. My husband uses it way more than me, but honestly, it is great being able to just go into the room for a bit, workout, shower in my own bathroom and waltz back into my office at any time.

- **Sole F85 treadmill** - I wish the screen could be used to play videos from my phone; as it is, it only shows stats. I also wish the shortcut buttons for incline and speed were linear, like, give me all the numbers from 1 to 15 instead of jumping from 1 to 3 to 6... Going up is too much of a speed jump, going down almost stalls the machine. And then I need to use the tiny buttons on the arm handles to go from 3 to 6 in decimal increments: 3.1, 3.2, 3.3....4.8... 5.9...6. Ridiculous. Apart from that, its a pretty good machine.
- **Inspire FT2** with the accessories pack, bench and leg machine - It is great and does almost everything.
