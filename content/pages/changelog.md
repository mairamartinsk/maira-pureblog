The design went through several changes over the years, but I found a screenshot of the final iteration on the Wayback Machine.

From 2005 to 2012 I have blogged under several different domain names, using Blogger/Blogspot and Wordpress, mostly about my life -- college, moving out of home, moving to a bigger city, moving to a different country, getting married, getting a dog...
